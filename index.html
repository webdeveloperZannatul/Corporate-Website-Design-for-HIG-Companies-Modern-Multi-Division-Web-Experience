Excellence in Property Management

HIG Property Management delivers comprehensive management services that maximize asset value and enhance resident experience.

With over 25 years of experience in property management, HIG Property Management has established itself as a trusted partner for property owners, investors, and housing authorities. Our team brings together expertise in operations, finance, compliance, and customer service to deliver exceptional results across diverse property types.

We manage a diverse portfolio of residential and commercial properties, including affordable housing communities, market-rate apartments, hotels, and mixed-use developments. Our management approach is data-driven, resident-focused, and tailored to the specific needs of each property and owner.

Our commitment to operational excellence is reflected in our industry-leading occupancy rates, resident satisfaction scores, and asset performance metrics. We combine proven management systems with innovative technology to streamline operations, reduce costs, and enhance the living experience for residents.

Our Team

HIG Property Management is led by a team of seasoned professionals with extensive experience across all aspects of property operations. Our leadership includes certified property managers, compliance specialists, financial analysts, and customer service experts who work together to deliver exceptional results.

Our Experience

We currently manage over 150 properties totaling more than 10,000 units across multiple states. Our portfolio includes affordable housing developments, market-rate apartments, senior living communities, hotels, and commercial properties. This diverse experience gives us unique insights into best practices across different property types and market conditions.

Our Approach

We believe that successful property management requires a balanced approach that considers the needs of owners, residents, and staff. We focus on creating value through operational efficiency, proactive maintenance, strategic capital planning, and exceptional customer service. Our management philosophy emphasizes transparency, accountability, and continuous improvement.
